However, a large amount of this new information we’re exposed to today also trains the pleasure centers of our brains to look for more of the same,- not unlike an addiction. In fact, that anxiety we feel when we’re away from our devices too long is related to withdrawal symptoms associated with substance abuse.  There is a physiological response that people have to opioid painkillers and as a society we try to design remediations based on that understanding. Similarly, the brain can come to crave the affirmation and stimulation that is most prevalent in social media. Years after having changed to other phones, the reaction to what was once your phone’s ringtone ☏ can cause a strong startled reaction. There’s a strong Pavlovian response-reward trigger within the human brain.
