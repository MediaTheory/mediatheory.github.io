Where we see a semi-chronological list of stories and posts from friends, and the designers and researchers at Facebook and Snapchat and Twitter are sending us addictive stimula. The design choices are not to give us updates on our friends’ successes and thoughts, but rather to keep us engaged, keep us from looking away. The algorithm for your feed is designed to never let you catch up. Fear of missing out on your friends’ lives forces you to stay even more engaged in social media. You are bringing a linear interest to a game and behavioral theory fight.
