This isn’t meant to say that all digital communication and social media channels are vapid and degrading society. There are many positive roles social media plays including staying close with far flung friends and professional contacts and being a source of communication beyond “official” channels as seen in the Arab Spring and various political uprisings. But the research shows that there are valid neurochemical changes and concerns that one should take into account as the balance of information takes place. Pizza is a favorite food of many but its cheese targets the same neurological dopamine filled pleasure centers as drugs and digital content. We know that we can’t live off a diet of pizza for breakfast, lunch, and dinner without serious health consequences. The same goes for amounts and types of digital content.
